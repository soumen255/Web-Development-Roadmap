Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 4 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup fried onions
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • Whole spices:
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 4 cloves
    • 2 cinnamon sticks
    • 4 cardamom pods
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Ground spices:
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tbsp red chili powder
    • 1 tbsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and ground spices for 1 hour.
  3. Heat ghee in a large pot and add whole spices.
  4. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  7. Layer half of the parboiled rice over the chicken.
  8. Sprinkle half of the mint, coriander, and fried onions.
  9. Add remaining rice and top with remaining herbs and fried onions.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Chef's Tips

  • Use aged basmati rice for the best results.
  • Don't overcook the rice before layering.
  • Let the biryani rest before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  • Serve with raita and salad for a complete meal.
