Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Pad Thai sauce:
    • 3 tbsp fish sauce
    • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken and cook until done.
  5. Push ingredients to one side, crack eggs and scramble.
  6. Add drained noodles and sauce, stir well.
  7. Add bean sprouts and spring onions.
  8. Garnish with crushed peanuts and lime wedges.
  9. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Don't overcook the noodles during soaking.
  • Have all ingredients prepped before starting to cook.
  • Adjust sauce ingredients to taste.
