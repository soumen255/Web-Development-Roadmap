Ingredients

  • 1kg beef bones (marrow and knuckle)
  • 500g beef brisket
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 5cm piece ginger, sliced
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 star anise
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cardamom pod
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 200g rice noodles
  • Toppings:
    • Bean sprouts
    • Thai basil
    • Coriander leaves
    • Lime wedges
    • Thinly sliced beef
    • Hoisin sauce
    • Sriracha sauce

Instructions

  1. Char onion and ginger over open flame or under broiler.
  2. Toast spices in a dry pan until fragrant.
  3. Rinse bones and brisket, then parboil for 5 minutes.
  4. Rinse bones and brisket again, then add to large pot.
  5. Add 4L water, charred onion, ginger, and spices.
  6. Simmer for 3-4 hours, skimming foam occasionally.
  7. Remove brisket after 1.5 hours, slice thinly.
  8. Strain broth and season with fish sauce and sugar.
  9. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  10. Assemble bowls with noodles, broth, and toppings.

Chef's Tips

  • Take time to skim the broth for a clear soup.
  • Use fresh herbs for the best flavor.
  • Adjust seasoning to taste with fish sauce and sugar.
