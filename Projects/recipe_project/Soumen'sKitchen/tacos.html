Street Tacos

Ingredients

  • 500g flank steak or skirt steak
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa of choice
  • Marinade:
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
    • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Place steak in marinade and refrigerate for 1-4 hours.
  3. Heat grill or pan to high heat.
  4. Cook steak for 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  5. Let steak rest for 5 minutes, then slice thinly.
  6. Warm tortillas on the grill or in a dry pan.
  7. Assemble tacos with steak, onion, and cilantro.
  8. Serve with lime wedges and salsa.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh corn tortillas for authentic flavor.
  • Don't overcook the steak - it should be juicy.
  • Double up the tortillas to prevent breaking.
